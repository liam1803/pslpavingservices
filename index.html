Welcome to PSL Paving Services

PSL Paving is a family run buisness offering a comprehensive range of repair and restoration services on a wide variety of paving materials for any application. We can provide solutions for any area whether it be a driveway, patio, walkway courtyard, or car park and we can work with a wide range of materials ranging from standard block paving through to concrete or tarmac. We offer a reliable, friendly and personal service and we operate throughout the whole of Nottinghamshire and the surronding area.

Cleaning and Restoration

We offer specialised cleaning and restoration services which enable us to restore an old or badly stained area to its former glory removing weeds, moss, debris and stains along the way. Once restored, your block paving is sealed and finished to enable a higher resistance to future damage from the weather or everyday usage. These services are avaliable on a wide range of materials including tarmacadam, block paving and imprinted concrete drives and driveways.

Repairs and Alterations

You may have a paved patio, drive or driveway which is damaged or in need of resizing, extending or some other form of alteration , we are able to carry out alterations and repairs on all types of paving.

Clean Only Service

You may not need extensive restoration or repair services, you may just wish to keep your patio, drive or driveway as clean as it was the day it was installed. For you there is our clean only service.

New Installations

We are able to install new paving for any use and in the same wide range of materials. As with all of our oprations the install service is totally personalised and the work will be carried out by the buisness properitor. We NEVER sub-contract.
